PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)Product Name: Word of WowVersion: 2.0Date: 2026-02-25Founder: Aaditya Sharma1. Executive SummaryWord of Wow is a B2B SaaS platform that enables brands to scale trusted word-of-mouth marketing through an AI-powered, trust-gated advocacy system.Unlike traditional affiliate platforms such as CJ Affiliate or Impact where anyone can monetize instantly, Word of Wow requires advocates to earn monetization rights through a structured three-level progression:Explorer (Trust Builder)Advocate (Gated Affiliate)Partner (Elite Performance Advocate)The system verifies content authenticity, detects fraud, scores quality, and unlocks monetization progressively based on merit and performance.It does not just focus on getting direct sales with the link, but put a lot emphasis on how much the brand is being talked about on socials.The platform enables brands to:Be talked about more onlineEnsure those conversations are authenticReward high-quality advocatesMeasure real business impactUnlike traditional affiliate or influencer systems, Word of Wow uses a trust-gated 3-tier advocacy ladder to ensure that monetization is earned, not granted.2. Core MissionPrimary Goal:Increase meaningful, authentic online brand mentions at scale.Secondary Goals:Maintain platform trust integrityPrevent spam and artificial engagementProvide measurable ROICreate a merit-based advocacy economy3. Product StrategyWord of Wow operates on three pillars:Conversation Growth EngineTrust & Fraud InfrastructureProgressive Monetization SystemConversation volume without trust = spam.Trust without incentives = limited scale.Incentives without control = affiliate chaos.This system balances all three.4. User Roles4.1 AdvocateCreates content about brands and progresses through tiers.4.2 BrandLaunches campaigns to increase online mentions and/or conversions.4.3 AdminOversees fraud review, disputes, tier management, and compliance.5. 3-Tier Advocacy LadderThis is the core differentiator.🟢 Level 1 — Explorer (Trust Building)Purpose:Increase brand mentions while filtering low-quality participants.Capabilities:Participate in mention-based campaignsSubmit Reddit / LinkedIn URLsReceive AI score breakdownEarn store credits / perksBuild Trust ScoreRestrictions:No referral linksNo commissionsUnlock Requirements for Level 2:Minimum approved submissionsAuthenticity score above system thresholdFraud risk below thresholdConsistent posting behaviorGoal:Grow brand conversations safely and build advocate credibility.🔵 Level 2 — Advocate (Gated Monetization)Purpose:Monetize proven advocates without opening spam floodgates.Capabilities:Unique referral linksEarn capped commissionsAccess conversion dashboardParticipate in higher-value campaignsRestrictions:Commission capsLink usage policyMandatory disclosure enforcementUnlock Requirements for Level 3:Revenue generation benchmarkConversion qualityContinued trust complianceBrand feedback scoreGoal:Align performance with authenticity.🟣 Level 3 — Partner (Elite Tier)Purpose:Create a curated high-trust advocacy network.Capabilities:Higher commission %Revenue bonusesEarly campaign accessBrand collaboration opportunitiesVerified Partner badgeSelection:Top revenue percentileHigh engagement qualityZero fraud flagsStrong brand satisfactionGoal:Offer brands premium advocacy supply.6. Brand Campaign System6.1 Campaign ObjectivesBrands select campaign focus:Awareness (maximize mentions)Engagement (maximize meaningful interaction)Conversion (maximize revenue)BalancedSystem auto-adjusts recommended scoring weights.6.2 Brand Customization with GuardrailsBrands can adjust progression emphasis, but within system-enforced limits.Two-Layer Model:Layer 1 — Non-Negotiable (Platform-Controlled)Cannot be changed:Minimum authenticity thresholdMaximum fraud risk allowedDisclosure complianceAnti-spam guardrailsMinimum account ageLayer 2 — Adjustable (Brand-Controlled Within Ranges)Brands can adjust:Engagement weightConversion weightBrand match emphasisConsistency importanceBut:Authenticity floor remains lockedFraud thresholds remain lockedTotal weight must remain normalizedThis ensures flexibility without sacrificing trust integrity.7. Core System ArchitectureFrontend:React-based UIBackend:Node-based APIDatabase:PostgreSQL (Supabase)AI Engine:Authenticity analysisSemantic similarity detectionSpam classificationEngagement quality modelingQueue System:Asynchronous scoring + fraud processingPayments:Stripe ConnectTracking:UTM-based attribution + coupon tracking + API conversion integration8. Submission & Scoring FlowAdvocate submits URLSystem fetches content via official APIsContent analyzed across categoriesFraud detection executedComposite score calculatedTrust Score updatedReward distributed (if approved)9. Scoring FrameworkAll metrics normalized 0–100.Categories:Content QualityBrand RelevanceAuthenticityEngagement QualityAudience RelevanceFraud PenaltyFinal Score = Weighted Composite − Penalty Adjustments10. Trust Score SystemPersistent advocate-level metric.Calculated from:Historical authenticityFraud historyEngagement consistencyConversion impact (if Level 2+)Brand feedbackTrust Score:Visible to brandsUsed for tier progressionInfluences campaign eligibility11. Fraud Detection EngineIncludes:Embedding similarity clusteringStylometric fingerprint analysisEngagement anomaly detectionAccount health scoringCross-user coordination detectionConsequences:WarningReward freezeTier downgradeSuspensionPermanent ban12. Revenue Attribution SystemFor Level 2+ advocates:Unique referral linksUTM parametersCoupon associationConversion API integrationPost-purchase survey captureBrand ROI Metrics:Revenue generatedCampaign costNet profitROASRevenue per advocateLTV comparison13. Dashboards13.1 Advocate DashboardCurrent TierTrust ScoreSubmission historyScore breakdownEarnings summaryTier progression tracker13.2 Brand DashboardTotal mentions generatedA big separate total mentions list and numbers with comparison etc. that brand has all over the internet, on all social media platforms, comparisons on which platform it is being talked the most, a whole tracking system.Platform breakdownEngagement analyticsTrust distribution of advocatesConversion & revenue metricsROI calculatorFraud alertsPrimary visible KPI:Brand Conversation Growth Rate13.3 Admin DashboardFraud queueTier overrideManual review toolsCampaign compliance audit14.
